Method to the madness.

I believe that it is through my unique process of interpreting and understanding a brief, that my original approach to ideas comes from. My research usually begins at www.thesaurus.com, followed by a yellow highlighter and then an A3 white page accompanied by a chunky black marker. It’s here that my ideas hail from.
I don’t feel I have one distinct visual style (at least yet!) but rather bold ideas rooted in clever research and content. Usual comments about my work include:

-great work ethic
-excellent communication
-coherent process
