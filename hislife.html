Work
On the big screen


I was working on the film title for the documentary
“His life, His Horse and Me”. The documentary is based on the journey a father and his daughter take to places from his past, with an aim for his daughter to discover and fully understand where he comes from. I focused on the themes of nostalgia and journey. My image-making came from an old camcorder and a dashcam.



The calling card is made up of a video box and character bible which includes a password to access the documentary online. The character bible includes informations on and photographs of the main characters in the documentary.



The print elements of the project extends the visual language of the title sequence. The poster and calling card both use footage from the camcorder, which is complimented by subtle graphic devices, taken from the interface of the camcorder, to ground and collate the visual language.





The title sequence is best experienced with the sound on.
