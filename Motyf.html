Work
Designing for designers


MOTYF is a Moving Type Festival that was held in Dublin in 2018. I got the chance to submit my multidisciplinary branding for the event. My work was created on the concept of the future of typography. My work has a coded visual language, with polite AI undertones.
A type-only solution created with intriguing copy writing.







The fluorescent paper would be dynamically paired with a coloured light display projected on the exterior and interior of the exhibition.






The schedule would be filled with the finest type designers from Ireland and afar. MoTyf took place in the Lexicon in Dun Laoighaire, Dublin.
